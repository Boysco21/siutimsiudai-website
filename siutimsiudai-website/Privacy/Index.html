English

1. Introduction

Welcome to Siu Tim Siu Dai (少甜少底) ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect basic profile information required for authentication.
  • Dietary and Log Data: We collect the text, recipes, and food logs you input into the app to provide meal estimations and recipe substitutions.
  • Device and Usage Data: We collect standard diagnostic data to ensure the app functions correctly and securely.

3. Artificial Intelligence and Third-Party Processing (Important)

To provide core app functionality, such as smart recipe substitutions and meal macro estimation, we use artificial intelligence.

  • Google Cloud Integration: The text and dietary logs you input are sent to and processed by Google Cloud (our AI infrastructure provider).
  • Data Training: Your personal data and food logs are strictly used to process your immediate request and are not used by Google Cloud to train public AI models.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You have the right to delete your data at any time. You can instantly wipe all local device data and delete your server account by navigating to the Profile tab in the app and selecting Delete Account.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at clhb2116@gmail.com.

繁體中文

1. 簡介

歡迎使用少甜少底(Siu Tim Siu Dai)(下稱「我們」)。我們致力保護您的私隱。本私隱政策說明,當您使用我們的流動應用程式時,我們如何收集、使用及分享您的個人資料。

2. 我們收集的資料

  • 帳戶資料:當您建立帳戶時,我們可能會收集身分驗證所需的基本個人檔案資料。
  • 飲食及記錄資料:我們會收集您在應用程式內輸入的文字、食譜及飲食記錄,以提供餐膳估算及食譜替代建議。
  • 裝置及使用資料:我們會收集標準診斷資料,以確保應用程式正常及安全運作。

3. 人工智能及第三方處理(重要)

為提供核心功能(例如智能食譜替代及餐膳營養估算),我們會使用人工智能。

  • Google Cloud 整合:您輸入的文字及飲食記錄會傳送至 Google Cloud(我們的人工智能基礎設施供應商)並由其處理。
  • 資料訓練:您的個人資料及飲食記錄僅用於處理您當下的請求;Google Cloud 不會使用這些資料來訓練公開的人工智能模型。

4. 資料保留及刪除

我們僅會在提供服務所需的期間內保留您的資料。您有權隨時刪除您的資料。您可於應用程式的「個人檔案」分頁選擇「刪除帳戶」,即時清除所有本機裝置資料並刪除您的伺服器帳戶。

5. 聯絡我們

如您對本私隱政策有任何疑問,請透過電郵 clhb2116@gmail.com 與我們聯絡。
